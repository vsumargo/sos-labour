About Us

Originally our idea blossomed back in the end of 2019 before the world turned upside down and we were temporarily thrown into chaos. Now more than ever we realise that due to the booming construction industry, good trades are far and few, and more so, hard to pin down.

That’s why we created JIFFY.

To help construction companies find the required workforce they need, even on short notice. This way they achieve a quick, easy and effective solution that won’t cost a fortune either. We do this by presenting companies with a list of labourers from various fields which they can then connect with directly. Not only does this save contractors time, but also provides flexibility and more work opportunities for labourers allowing them to arrange their schedules and increase exposure to contractors.
